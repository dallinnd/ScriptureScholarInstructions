Welcome to the Scripture Scholar App

The primary purpose of this app is to assist you in your study of the official Canon of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, referred to as the Standard Works.

The first four sections are divided into steps:

  • Navigating the App
  • Searching the Standard Works
  • Saving Scriptures with Bookmarks
  • Viewing Chapters and Saved Verses
